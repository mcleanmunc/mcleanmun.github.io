12 Districts (Hunger Games)

Chair

Chloe Gao

Chloe Gao is a current sophomore at Mclean High School, and will be chairing the Hunger Games Crisis committee at McMUNC VI. This year will be her fourth year participating in MUN. For Chloe, MUN has been a way to learn about problems that either have or still are affecting millions of people globally. She also learned many skills that cannot be taught through a textbook. Skills like collaboration, communication, and leadership. Aside from MUN, Chloe fills her time playing volleyball, practicing a new song for orchestra, or learning a new programming algorithm. As someone who has received the many benefits MUN can give, Chloe hopes to see delegates seizing this opportunity to further develop their own leadership and communications skills. Skills that will lead delegates to countless opportunities in the future. Feel free to reach out to her at chloejgao@gmail.com.


Czar

Vidya Suri

Vidya Suri is a sophomore at McLean High School in her second year as a member of McLean’s Model United Nations Team. She really enjoys crisis committees, and Model UN has become a large part of her life. She also plays recreational soccer, frequently volunteers at her local library, is a member of the school’s Latin club, and participates in the Girls Who Code Club. In her spare time, Vidya loves reading, baking, and playing the piano. She is immensely excited to be the czar of the Hunger Games committee at this year’s McMUNC VI conference, and so implores delegates to email her with any questions that they may have at vidyamouse@gmail.com.
