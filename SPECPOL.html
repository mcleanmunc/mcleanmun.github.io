SPECPOL

Special Political and Decolonization Committee

Chair

Odessa Zhang

Odessa Zhang is currently a sophomore at McLean High School and has been doing Model UN since she was in seventh grade. She is delighted to welcome you all to SPECPOL this coming fall! Odessa has been a passionate member of the McLean MUN team; MUN has enabled her to become more confident and skilled in leadership, research, and public speaking. Outside of MUN, Odessa is a cross country athlete and founder of her own public speaking organization called Speak to Empower (STEP). You can also find her swiping her life away on TikTok, hanging out with friends, and binge-watching Netflix shows. Odessa encourages all middle school delegates to not stress and to feel free to reach out to her at odessa.zhang@gmail.com if there are any concerns. Good luck!
