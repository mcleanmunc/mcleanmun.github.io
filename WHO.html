Chair

Yanni Aknine

Yanni Aknine is a junior at McLean High School and is elated to chair the World Health Organization for aspiring middle school delegates. Undergoing his first year in Model United Nations, Yanni has been able to transform himself as a speaker who is becoming more involved in global affairs. Outside of Model UN, he participates in various clubs such as Debate, Sources of Strength, McWelcomers, and several other organizations. When not doing school work, Yanni spends his time hanging out with friends, watching every single show 10 times, going to new places, and many other activities! As a result of participating in several clubs involving teamwork and positivity, Yanni truly values diplomacy in order to create an enriching environment that fosters growth and development! He could not be more eager to hear all of the different stances and unique solutions presented by the promising future of Model UN! He wishes the best of luck to all of the delegates and cannot wait to meet all of them this fall to make it the best McMUNC yet! If there are any questions whatsoever, please feel free to email him at yanni.aknine@gmail.com.
