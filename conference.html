Our COVID-19 Response

Due to the unfortunate risks of COVID-19, the McMUNC VI secretariat has unanimously voted to hold McMUNC VI virtually. Despite the conferences online-nature, the secretariat is working tirelessly to ensure that this year's McMUNC experience is still representative of conferences in the past. We hope to see the same passion, innovation, and diplomacy that we would experience in person and can't wait for all delegates to undergo this year's unique simulation.

F.A.Q

Some common concerns with Virtual Conferences

  • What will Virtual McMUNC VI be like?

    The McMUNC VI Secretariat is working hard to try to keep the same, amazing McMUNC expereience as seen in past years and we're confident that Virtual McMUNC VI will be just as much of an awarding and excellent experience. Ultimately, due to the current health concerns, we've been forced to move to a virtual platform. Despite this, we're continuing to navigate through this unprecedented time and we're confident that we can provide the same experience virtually as in-person. Instead of gathering together at McLean HS, we'll be in the comfort of our own homes, enjoying our own snacks while discussing current events.

    If you have any further questions regarding Virtual McMUNC VI, please do not hesitate to contact our Vice-Director of Technology, Andrew Zhao. zhao.andrew@gmail.com

  • Is there anything I should know about registering for Virtual McMUNC VI?

    Virtual McMUNC VI registration will cost $##.##. All delegates will have until the deadline to register, which is TBD. We understand that everyone has their own unique situation however we encourage everyone to register as early as possible to let us know that you will be attending! By doing so, we can better prepare for the upcoming conference.

  • Do I still need to register with the other delegates from my school?

    Should multiple delegates from the same school wish to register for McMUNC VI, we ask that they all register together as one delegation. However, if the delegate is the only one wishing to register from their school, they must register with a parent/guardian as a point of contact.

  • What technological and/or interent capabilies that I would need to attend?

    We strongly recommend that delegates attend McMUNC VI on an internet connected laptop. However, any device running either Mac, Windows, Linux, iOS, or Android will be sufficient to attend this conference. For those who have it, we further recommend the use of an ethernet cable to ensure connectivity.

    Should you find yourself unable to connect or experiencing issues with connectivity, please email your chair immediately to work out a solution.

  • Is there still a chance McMUNC VI will be in-person?

    Unfortunately, due to the uncertainty surrounding the evolving pandemic, McMUNC VI will be hosted virtually. Our goal is to ensure the health and safety of our participants and hopefully return to in-person conferences next year.

  • Will there still be awards?

    Yes! Awards will be delivered by mail and should the winning delegate be unreachable, we will mail it to their respective MUN advisors.
