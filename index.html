A note from our Secretary-General

Esteemed Sponsors and Delegates,


On behalf of the entire Secretariat, welcome to the Sixth Iteration of the McLean Model United Nations Conference! The McMUNC VI Staff and Secretariat have been working around the clock for the past several months to ensure the best possible delegate experience. Although McMUNC VI will be hosted virtually this year, we still hope to uphold the McMUNC VI tradition of excitement, problem-solving, and healthy competition!

We are honored to host one of the first Model UN conferences of the school year and cannot wait to aid in the introduction of this amazing experience to all your novice delegates. Model UN has been one of the most transformative experiences I’ve had in my life, and I hope McMUNC VI will be one of the many stops along your MUN journey.

This year, McMUNC VI is proud to host 18 robust committees ranging from the 1953 Iranian Coup D’État to the Asia Cooperation Dialogue.

McMUNC IV would not be possible without the help of all our sponsors and delegates. Thank you for everything that you do. Our staff and secretariat hope to make this a weekend an unforgettable experience filled with excitement, memories, and healthy competition.


Sincerely,

Kyla Duggal

Secretary-General of McMUNC VI
