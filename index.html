Our COVID-19 Response

Our COVID-19 Response

Due to the unfortunate risks of COVID-19, the McMUNC VI secretariat has unanimously voted to hold McMUNC VI virtually. Despite the conferences online-nature, the secretariat is working tirelessly to ensure that this year's McMUNC experience is still representative of conferences in the past. We hope to see the same passion, innovation, and diplomacy that we would experience in person and can't wait for all delegates to undergo this year's unique simulation.
